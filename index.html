    How do I book a consultation with Dheeraj ji?⌄

    Simply click any "Book Now" button, fill in your name, your key concern and basic birth details (date, time, place of birth). You'll receive a WhatsApp message from Dheeraj ji within 2 hours to confirm your session. Payment is made securely via Razorpay before the session for paid services.

    What are the consultation fees? Are there hidden charges?⌄

    Absolutely no hidden charges. Services start at ₹599 (Gemstone Consultation), Kundali Reading is ₹799, Career Guidance ₹1,199, and our full comprehensive reading is ₹2,499. All prices include GST. You see the full price before payment. The free 5-min WhatsApp chat requires no payment at all.

    I don't know my exact birth time. Can I still get a reading?⌄

    Yes. Dheeraj ji can still provide valuable insights using your birth date, approximate time and place. For maximum accuracy, he uses birth time rectification techniques. The Kundali may be slightly adjusted, but Dasha analysis, Rashi and Nakshatra readings will still be meaningful. Please share the closest known time and mention it's approximate.

    Is this consultation available for NRIs and clients outside India?⌄

    Yes. All consultations are done online via WhatsApp message, WhatsApp call, or Zoom video call. Clients from USA, UK, Canada, Australia, UAE and other countries are regularly served. Remedies are always tailored to what is practically possible in your location — no requirement to visit India.

    What makes Apka Jyotish different from other astrologers?⌄

    Three things: (1) No fear-based predictions — Dheeraj ji focuses on empowerment and practical steps, not scary doom-saying. (2) Realistic remedies — nothing that costs thousands or requires you to buy specific products from him. (3) Follow-up included — all services come with a follow-up window so your doubts are actually resolved, not just answered once.

    Is there a refund policy?⌄

    If a session is unable to proceed due to an issue on our side, a full refund is issued. Consultations that have been completed are non-refundable as significant preparation time is invested. If you're unsatisfied, please share your feedback and Dheeraj ji will personally address your concern within the follow-up window.

    Is my information kept confidential?⌄

    Absolutely. Your birth details, personal concerns and session content are 100% confidential. This information is never shared with third parties. Your WhatsApp conversations are encrypted by default. Apka Jyotish treats client privacy with the same respect as a doctor-patient relationship.
